Research Interests

Networked systems, mobile computing, and wireless networking.

Currently focusing on mobile immersive computing (including 360-degree video streaming, volumetric video streaming, augmented reality, and virtual reality), 5G, Internet of Things (IoT), edge computing, and network management & automation.

Past research related to network functions virtualization, software defined networking, multipath TCP for mobile applications, mobile opportunistic networks, and 802.11 WiFi networks.
