Biography

Bo Han received the Ph.D. degree from the University of Maryland, the M.Phil. degree from City University of Hong Kong, and the Bachelor degree from Tsinghua University, all in computer science. He is a principal inventive scientist at AT&T Labs Research. His research interests are in the areas of mobile computing, wireless networking, and networked systems. He has published 60+ papers in prestigious international journals and conference proceedings. He is the co-inventor of 10+ U.S. patents. He received the best paper award from ACM CoNEXT 2016.
